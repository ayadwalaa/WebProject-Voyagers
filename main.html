Voyagers Shipping Company

Voyagers is a global shipping company that provides services in many different fields. It has a crew of +3000 members varying between captains, engineers, labor, chiefs, deck staff and maintenance members. It transports natural oil and gas, dry bulk carriers, heavy materials, fertilizers, containers and provides many different services for local citizens and tourists. Voyagers plays a very important role in the economic development of the USA. Voyagers vision is to achieve the most valuable impact with wiser consumption of resources. Voyagers' ships are environmentally friendly as possible and safety insurance for our crew has our highest priority which all lead to the most reliable transportation.

We recognize the responsibility we have towards our environment, and place great emphasis on the identification and mitigation of Health, Safety and Environment (HSE) risks. Each of our businesses are required to pass a stringent Health, Safety and Environmental Impact Assessment (HSEIA), and are subject to continuous environmental monitoring. We invest significantly in environmental projects, and continue to take steps to reduce flaring and Green House Gas (GHG) emissions. We are also responsible for a series of marine protection and development projects, including mangrove and seagrass plantation, coral reef rehabilitation, and the deployment of artificial reef and fish habitat structures.

Voyagers Shipping Company

Our people and contractors are at the heart of 100% HSE. They enforce and implement the Group HSE policy, identify and mitigate risks, and keep themselves and their colleagues safe. All ADNOC employees are empowered and obliged to intervene in unsafe situations, address the issue, and ensure that our policies and procedures are followed. Our people make sure that Abu Dhabi’s oil and gas industry remains one of the safest in the world, with a Lost Time Frequency Injury (LTIF) rate that is considerably lower than the global industry average. Yet there is no room for complacency: 100% HSE is a continuous and never-ending effort, whether we are at work, at home, or abroad. By embracing, believing in and being accountable for 100% HSE, we make sure everyone goes home safe every day.
